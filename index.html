Yuijit Kwong



I'm an essayist, poet, and musician based in the northeastern US. I write politically about music, art, gender, indigeneity, and urbanism, with an interest in how spaces are represented and how these representations inform and reflect our identities. A graduate of Harvard Divinity School, I studied these topics within Islamic contexts.

My writing has appeared in Ajam Media Collective, The Trinity Review, UC Review, and more. I may or may not perform in a city near you. Imagine it so.
